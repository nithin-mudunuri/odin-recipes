Air Fryer Quesadilla

Home

Description

Easy Quesadila Recipe you can make in your air fryer in just 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 Flour Tortillas
  • 1/2 cup Shredded Cheese
  • Nonstick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Heat tortillas in the microwave until pliable, about 15 seconds.
  2. Place 1/4 cup cheese on one half of each tortilla; fold other half of tortilla over cheese. Place in air fryer. Spray with non-stick cooking spray.
  3. Set temperature to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Place quesadillas in the basket of the air fryer, and cook until golden brown, 4 to 6 minutes.
  4. Flip quesadillas, press down with a spatula, and air fry an additional 2 to 3 minutes.
